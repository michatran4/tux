# sxiv
sxiv is the simple X image viewer.

## Usage
- sxiv [options] [file(s)]

## Options
- **-f** Opens in fullscreen mode.
- **-S [seconds]** Opens in slideshow mode, with a delay between images.
- **-z [percentage]** Zoom in.

*Command added on 2021-10-20*
